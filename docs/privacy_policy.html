Privacy Policy for SoloCraft Studio

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Introduction

SoloCraft Studio ("we", "our", or "the app") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding your information. Our app is designed to be fully local and private, meaning we do not require accounts and do not store your creative content on our servers.

1. No Personal Information Required

SoloCraft Studio does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal identity information to use the app. All features are available immediately without registration.

2. Content You Create

All music, recordings, and projects you create are stored locally on your device.

  • No Cloud Storage: We do not upload your recordings or project files to any servers.
  • User Control: You have total control over your data. If you delete a recording or project within the app, it is removed from your device.

3. Device Permissions

Microphone Permission

  • Purpose: To record audio and create music.
  • Usage: Used only when you are actively recording. Audio is saved only to your device.

Storage/Files Permission

  • Purpose: To save and load your musical projects.
  • Usage: To store project data and audio files locally.

Camera Permission (Optional)

  • Purpose: To capture photos for custom background themes.
  • Usage: Used only when you choose to take a photo for a theme.

4. Automatically Collected Data

We do not collect personal usage data. We may use basic anonymized crash reporting tools provided by the operating system (Google Play/Apple) to improve app stability. This data contains no personal identifiers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or rent any user data to third parties. Since we do not collect your data on our servers, there is no data to share.

6. Your Rights (Data Deletion)

You have full control over your data. You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Deleting individual projects or recordings within the app.
  • Using the "Clear All Data" feature in the app settings.
  • Uninstalling the app (which removes all locally stored app data).

7. Children's Privacy

Because we do not collect personal information or require accounts, the app is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy:
