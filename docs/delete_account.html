Data Deletion & Control

At SoloCraft Studio, your privacy is our priority. Since our app is designed to be fully local, we do not store your data on our servers. This means you have immediate and total control over your information.

How to Delete Your Data

You can remove all your creative work and settings at any time using the following methods:

1. Within the App

  • Delete Individual Items: Navigate to your Library or Projects and swipe/select items to delete them permanently.
  • Clear All Data: Go to the Profile screen and tap "Clear All Data". This will instantly reset the app and remove all local projects and recordings.

2. System Settings

  • Uninstalling: Removing the app from your device will delete all associated local data including projects, audio recordings, and custom themes.
  • Clear App Cache/Data: You can also use your device's system settings (Android/iOS) to clear the storage for SoloCraft Studio.

No Server-Side Data

Because we do not require accounts and do not use cloud synchronization, there is no data on our servers for us to delete. Once you delete it from your device, it is gone forever.

For any other privacy concerns, please read our Privacy Policy or contact us at support@ujstudios.com.
