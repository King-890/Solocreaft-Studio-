Account Deletion Request

At SoloCraft Studio, we respect your right to control your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

To request the deletion of your account and data, please send an email to our support team:

Email: support@ujstudios.com

Subject Line: Account Deletion Request - [Your Username]

Please include the following in your email:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your username (if applicable)
  • A statement confirming you want to permanently delete your account

What Happens Next?

  • Our team will verify your ownership of the account.
  • Once verified, we will permanently delete your account and all associated personal data (recordings, projects, profile info) from our servers.
  • This process may take up to 30 days.

Data Retention

We may retain certain anonymized data for analytical purposes or data required by law, as outlined in our Privacy Policy.
